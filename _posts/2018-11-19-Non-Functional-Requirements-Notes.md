NFRs are essentially those important cogs in the wheel that appear to be trivial but have a bigger impact if ignored.

- Scalability  
- Performance  
- High Availability  
- Security  
- Usability  
- Internationalization and Localization  
- Browser Compatibility  
- Compliance  
- Accessibility
